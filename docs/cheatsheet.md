# Cheatsheet

A one-page quick reference to the AI storytelling vices and the techniques that fix them. Two audiences: writers who generate fiction **with** an AI in a chat window, and developers who **build** AI-driven narrative tools. The "Quick fix" column is for the first; the "Systematic fix" column is for the second.

Start with the [core idea](the-core-idea.md): stubborn prose vices are not killed by stacking more "don't do X" rules. The durable fix is making the model re-state an affirmative commitment right before it writes. The gain comes from the reflexive re-read, not from self-detection. See the [glossary](glossary.md) for shared terms.

## Prose & dialogue vices

| Vice | The tell | Quick fix (writing with AI) | Systematic fix (building a tool) | Doc |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Contrastive Reveal (Not X, but Y) | A clause negates a plausible alternative before asserting the "true" one, staged as a twist you never actually held. Cut the "not X" half; if it still works, it was decoration. | Delete the decoy and state the real thing directly, backed by a concrete image. Ask for a rewrite of any negate-to-reveal sentence. | Required self-audit field with an affirmative pledge; back with a regex-and-regenerate check on negation markers; fuse with sibling retraction vices. | [Contrastive reveal](vices/contrastive-reveal.md) |
| Recap Chain | Beats open by re-listing prior state ("having done X, you now Y") instead of advancing. Strike the first sentence; if nothing is lost, it was recap. | Tell the model to start each beat on the new event and assume you remember prior turns; have characters react to the want, not a transcript. | Schema self-audit scoped by declared prose_kind so real summaries aren't flagged; keyword-overlap check for playback; don't feed the writer its own raw prose. | [Recap chain](vices/recap-chain.md) |
| Dialogue Fragmentation | Three to five short clauses fired in a row, each slammed shut by a full stop where a comma or dash belonged. Read aloud; if you inhale at every period, it's fragmented. | Ask the model to connect clauses that elaborate one idea; describe a terse character as "few, well-chosen words," never "speaks in fragments." | Reflexive cadence gate fused with sibling cadence vices; guard the memory channel so one staccato scene doesn't teach the rest. | [Dialogue fragmentation](vices/dialogue-fragmentation.md) |
| Gesture Gloss | A gesture is shown, then a trailing clause translates its meaning ("as if / like someone who / a sign of"). Delete everything after the gesture; if the action carries it, the gloss was caption. | Show gestures from the outside and stop; let meaning land in the detail or another character's reaction. Keep physical similes. | Schema self-audit plus post-generation regex on high-confidence markers that triggers regeneration; strip upstream "inner voice" prompt language. | [Gesture gloss](vices/gesture-gloss.md) |
| Aphorism & Oracle Voice | Beats cap with portable maxims; characters speak in branded proverbs. Pillow test: if a line would survive cross-stitched on a pillow, it's an aphorism. | End beats on a concrete image, not a sentence naming its meaning; ground reserved characters in "few words about something concrete." | Reflexive `no_aphorism` gate; remove upstream "resonant endings" pressure; add a reviewer that flags copula-plus-abstract final sentences. | [Aphorism & oracle voice](vices/aphorism-and-oracle.md) |
| Narrate-then-un-narrate | The narrator asserts a fact then retracts it ("maybe a branch; it was not a branch"; "or rather"). Two versions of a fact, first canceled. | Tell the model to write the narrator as certain: state each fact once and stand behind it. Redirect false mystery into accumulating detail. | Pre-emit `commit_to_the_frame` gate fused with contrastive reveal; regex-and-regenerate on hedging connectives; give the writer concrete grounding. | [Narrate-then-un-narrate](vices/narrate-then-unnarrate.md) |
| Onomatopoeia Spam | Sound words on nearly every beat, often alone on a line, doing the sentence's work. More than ~one standalone per paragraph is spam. | Instruct affirmatively: one sound word per beat at most, folded into the sentence; frame as parsimony, not a ban. Re-license exclamation energy if the model went flat. | Do NOT use a suppressor gate (it hardens into a total ban). Handle excess after generation with a density-counting reviewer; never plant a literal sound word. | [Onomatopoeia spam](vices/onomatopoeia-spam.md) |
| Uniform Laconic Voice | The whole cast speaks in one clipped, withholding register; warm characters come out brooding; punctuation cools over a long run. | Anchor voice to temperament, not mood; make expansive the default; tell the model not to inherit the volume of recent turns. | Ground the character generator in real varied exemplars; reflexive voice gate; stop long-run decay at the memory layer (anti-autophagy). | [Uniform laconic voice](vices/uniform-laconic-voice.md) |
| Speaking for the Protagonist / Reader | The model invents the POV character's dialogue, choices, and feelings the input never contained. Input is the ceiling, not the seed. | Tell the model the input is the ceiling: render only the declared action; no dialogue in means the character acts in silence; end at the decision point. | Two-part pre-emit gate (no invented POV dialogue; input-as-ceiling); feed the writer the protagonist's identity snapshot; reviewer reads full scene state. | [Speaking for the protagonist](vices/speaking-for-the-protagonist.md) |
| Voice Homogeneity | Strip the speaker tags; if you can't tell who said what, the voices collapsed. Amplitude compresses toward restraint. | Vary loudness, pace, warmth, vocabulary with expansive as default; give each speaker one verbal habit; ground on real verbatim excerpts. | Ground the generator on varied verbatim exemplars in the cached prefix; amplitude as its own field; per-character `voice_matches_person` gate; fix source not just renderer. | [Voice homogeneity](vices/voice-homogeneity.md) |
| Forced Sensory Detail | Tension triggers the same chemical cluster (ozone, copper, iron) the POV character could never name. Swap it into three unrelated scenes unedited and it's a stamp. | Draw every smell and taste from the character's own world; render force through physical effect, not invented chemistry. | Reflexive `sensory_from_the_world` gate; post-generation regex for chemistry tokens; rebalance register exemplars; don't feed the writer its own prose. | [Forced sensory detail](vices/forced-sensory-detail.md) |
| Epithet / Archetype Naming | Names that spell out the theme ("the Brooding Swordsman," "Marked by Sorrow"). Street test: would a real person say it aloud without a straight face? | Give plain, lived-in names; let action reveal character; keep secrets out of the name; run the street test on nicknames. | Encode accepted name forms affirmatively in the generation schema description; review pass re-names epithet templates; ground on varied real conventions. | [Epithet naming](vices/epithet-naming.md) |
| Register & Anachronism Leak | Wrong formality or modern slang leaks into a period voice ("no worries" from a knight); slow drift toward the contemporary default. | Name the register with a floor and ceiling; name forbidden tokens literally (the token exception); re-anchor against drift with an early good line. | State register affirmatively and name hard tokens; `register_holds` gate; redundant channel plus deterministic rewrite for a stubborn token; bound memory windows. | [Register & anachronism leak](vices/register-and-anachronism-leak.md) |

## Structural & worldbuilding vices

| Vice | The tell | Quick fix (writing with AI) | Systematic fix (building a tool) | Doc |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Tonal Drift (Grimdark Creep) | Sentences look fine but the aggregate cools: wonder narrated as a briefing, marvels shrugged at, punctuation flattening. Compare an early scene to a late one. | Name the target register concretely; check your examples actually demonstrate warmth; de-grim input seeds; don't feed the model its own recent prose. | Rebalance exemplars toward the intended register (real verbatim over invented); apply register discipline at every authoring stage; break the loop at the memory layer; prefer subtractive fixes. | [Tonal drift](vices/tonal-drift.md) |
| Self-Cannibalizing Repetition (Autophagy) | Fed its own prose, the model amplifies its habits: calcified imagery, narrowing vocabulary, punctuation sliding to zero across a session. | Don't paste the model's recent prose back as a style to match; give a factual recap; re-anchor to an early excerpt; keep an "already used" list. | Architecture fix: current-scene-only prose window (reset at scene cuts), distilled factual summaries for continuity, an overused-imagery ledger fed back as vary-these. | [Self-cannibalizing repetition](vices/self-cannibalizing-repetition.md) |
| Everything Is a Crisis | Every scene defaults to threat or extortion; idle beats get seeded with menace; place-texture starves. Variety test: list the last five drivers. | Name the calm register; ask for a stakes rotation with no repeated conflict type; request place-texture; ask for a no-menace beat as a diagnostic. | Register discipline on the planner; required `scene_driver` forcing function (rest/trade/wonder are legitimate); wire recent-conflict history into every path. | [Everything is a crisis](vices/everything-is-a-crisis.md) |
| Inert World | The world only reacts, never initiates; every change traces to the protagonist. Empty-room test and come-back test both show nothing moving. | Ask for at least one thing per scene the character didn't cause; time passes for everyone; add a self-check for off-screen change. | First diagnose the wiring gap. Add a required `world_pulse` forcing function on the planner (advance-or-hold, with hold legitimate); avoid a deterministic scheduled tick. | [Inert world](vices/inert-world.md) |

## Techniques

The fixes above draw on a shared toolkit. Each doc covers a manual chat version and a systematic build version.

| Technique | Summary | Doc |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Reflexive Schema Gates | The required affirmative-pledge audit field whose payoff is the pre-write re-read, not self-detection. | [Schema gates](techniques/schema-gates.md) |
| No Pink Elephant | Never quote the bad pattern in an instruction; naming it makes it salient. State rules affirmatively; keep literal bad examples out of prompts and enums. | [No pink elephant](techniques/no-pink-elephant.md) |
| Principles Over Rules | A short affirmative principle plus anchored surface faces generalizes; long enumerated rule-lists overfit and leak. | [Principles over rules](techniques/principles-over-rules.md) |
| Examples Plant Their Vices | Few-shot examples transmit their own flaws; a mediocre sample caps quality and an invented one plants its tics. Default to principles-first, zero-shot. | [Examples plant their vices](techniques/no-vice-planting-examples.md) |
| Ground Voice in Real Exemplars | Steer voice and range with short verbatim passages of real published prose, chosen to span the axis the model collapses on. | [Ground voice in real exemplars](techniques/reference-real-exemplars.md) |
| Separate Planning From Prose | Split the factual planner (data, no prose gates) from the prose writer (renders it, carries all the gates). | [Separate planning from prose](techniques/separate-planning-from-prose.md) |
| Anti-Autophagy Memory | Stop the writer feeding on its own prose: distilled facts for continuity, a small scene-scoped raw window, a worn-imagery ledger. | [Anti-autophagy memory](techniques/anti-autophagy-memory.md) |
| Post-Hoc Validation and Regeneration | Catch semantic and excess vices with a detector after generation and regenerate, layered behind pre-write gates. | [Post-hoc validation and regeneration](techniques/post-hoc-validation-and-regen.md) |
| Second-Pass Reviewer | A separate reviewer that reads the whole work in scene with fresh records to catch continuity breaks, agency theft, and residual vices. | [Second-pass reviewer](techniques/second-pass-reviewer.md) |
| Testing Vices and Accepting Ceilings | Probe varied and blind scenarios to catch migration; measure real rates with an independent judge; accept the model's residual ceiling. | [Testing vices and ceilings](techniques/testing-vices-and-ceilings.md) |
